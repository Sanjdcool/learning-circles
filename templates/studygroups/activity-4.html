Sharing (15 min)

Try to strike a balance between learners sharing something personal that also directly relates to the course material. In the past, public speaking learners have shared speeches they love from YouTube, HTML/CSS learners have shared their favorite website, and Registered Nursing exam prep learners have shared embarrassing stories from nursing school.

  • First: Ask a learner to introduce a relevant resource/story and, if applicable, explain why the like it.
  • Next: Go around the room and hear from each of the other learners.
